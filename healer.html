Personal Project

I started this project around the beginning of May 2025. It was for a dear friend who cheered me during difficult times.
Perhaps my motivation is as inconsistent as the musical aspect of this project had already been done two months prior. Only then did I start making the video concept.

These are the original sketches for the video. It was based off of a TV show about dreams (at least that's what I think it was about). A lot of unused objects are displayed here.
Such as the magic wand, the striped little white tiger cub, the sockpuppets, and carousel-like building from the TV show.

Then, I sketched the character design and recreated it in Blender. After that, I chose the colors for her.

While I was recreating her outlines in with Grease Pencil in Blender, I also made the boat model. I used reference from the opening scene of the TV show.

I settled on an image for the stary sky background, and created the environment. Then I finalised the character's form.
I've also added a lamp to the boat and a geometry behind her to create a shadow.
Finally, it was time to make the constellations.

I had already sketched the constellations on paper, so what I had to do was to arrange them in the sky.
I did this in Paint.NET, using reference images of the subjects. It was quite fun adapting them into constellation forms.
Some were tricky to convert because I wanted to keep the details so that the subjects would be recognizable,
but I also needed them to be simple in order to look like constellations.

When all the art were ready to go, I rendered them all out into a sequence of images and edited them with the music.
And out came the final piece you see below:

This was the fastest I've ever completed a project (and I barely have any complete projects).
Except for the music, the entire work took under a week to finish.
I couldn't be more proud of the end result, and I'm happy that I've made something worthwhile.

Thank you for viewing my project.
